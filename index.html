Signal Profile

Your rules.
Your AI.

Every Sage app runs on your Signal Profile — the config that tells the AI who matters, what to flag, and what to permanently ignore. Update via Telegram /settings or the web. It never updates silently.

  • 🎯
    Priority senders
    BSE India, your bank, Kroll, your manager — always surface to the top regardless of email volume.
  • 🔔
    Alert keywords
    "action required", "OTP", "withdrawal deadline" — immediate Telegram push, no waiting for morning brief.
  • 🔇
    Noise filters
    Snapfish, Toastmasters, Michael Page — never surface again. Your list, your rules, permanent.
  • 🧠
    Gets smarter, stays yours
    Voluntary post-brief feedback. After 90 days it sounds like your own instincts. That trained profile belongs to you.
